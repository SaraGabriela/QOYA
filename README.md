# QOYA :Management suite
**The intelligent assistant that frees business owners’ time and boosts growth.**

---

## Problem
Business owners across LATAM lose hours every week managing suppliers, confirming payments, organizing receipts, and keeping their operations compliant.  
These administrative tasks drain time, cause errors, and slow down business growth.

---

## User
**Who Qoya serves:**  
Small and medium business owners (cafés, restaurantes, panaderías, tiendas locales) who:  
- Want to grow but are overwhelmed by operational tasks  
- Need reliable payment tracking  
- Want to reduce mistakes and save time  

**User Insight:**  
Administration feels like a burden that steals time from creativity, innovation, and sales.

---

## Solution
Qoya automates the operational backbone of a business.  
This module focuses specifically on:

### **Payment Automation + Blockchain Verification**
- Automatically processes payments to suppliers  
- Generates and stores each payment receipt  
- Converts each receipt into a **hash** stored on blockchain for immutable verification  
- Centralizes all confirmations in one secure dashboard  

Qora becomes the “always-on” assistant ensuring every transaction is recorded, real, and traceable.

---

## User Stories

### **Ana – Café in Lima, Peru**  
- Before: Manually paid suppliers and kept paper receipts in folders  
- After: Qoya pays suppliers automatically and stores every voucher in blockchain. Ana only reviews a daily summary.

### **Lucía – Ice Cream Shop in Buenos Aires, Argentina**  
- Before: Lost receipts and had payment disputes with one supplier  
- After: Qoya hashes every receipt and stores it immutably. Disputes dropped to zero.

### **Carlos – Restaurant in Bogotá, Colombia**  
- Before: Needed an accountant to track payments  
- After: Qoya generates a secure, organized payment history with blockchain timestamps.

### **María – Bakery in Mexico City, Mexico**  
- Before: Unclear which payments were completed  
- After: Qoya’s dashboard shows real-time status + verified proofs.

## Phases
Phase 1: Supplier Payment Module
1. Order Recording
- Stores details for each order: date, supplier, ingredients, quantities
2. Receipt Confirmation
- Users confirm supplies received
- Records total payable, payment deadlines, and supplier info
3. Payment Calculation
- Aggregates totals per supplier
- Schedules payments by due date
4. Wallet Balance Verification
- Checks business wallet balance
- Requests additional funds if insufficient
- Confirms payment initiation with the user
5. Payment Execution
- Automates payment transfer to suppliers
6. Confirmation & Receipt Storage
- Receives payment confirmations
- Stores receipts on-chain for verification
- Sends copies to suppliers
7. History & Auditing
- Maintains full payment history with statuses (success, errors)
  8. Dashboard / Visualization
- Wallet movements, total paid per period
- Supplier ranking by payments
- Quick insights for decision-making

## Roadmap / Future Vision
- Build on-chain credit scores from payment history
- Expand Qoya to other operations:
- Inventory management
- Promotion planning
- Accounting and bookkeeping
- Reduce operational costs and manual work for entrepreneurs

## Conclusion
Qoya acts as a virtual administrative assistant, automating repetitive tasks, ensuring reliable payments, and providing entrepreneurs with actionable insights to make faster, smarter business decisions. It helps small food businesses save time, reduce errors, and focus on growth, innovation, and customer experience.
